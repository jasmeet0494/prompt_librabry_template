# 📂 04 — Receivables Review and Follow-up Workflow

**Business function** : Finance / Working Capital / Collections Support
**Trigger**: Accounts receivable ageing report is available and overdue items need internal follow-up
**Prompts in this section**: P08, P10

---

# Section Purpose

This section supports the receivables review and collections follow-up workflow by helping finance teams identify overdue invoices and then prepare clear internal communication for issue resolution. The process begins with analysing the accounts receivable ageing report to flag overdue items, trace them to the relevant account, deal, service line, and responsible team, and prioritise the items needing action. It then uses that reviewed issue summary to draft a professional internal follow-up email requesting clarification, status, and expected resolution timelines. Together, these prompts reduce manual effort, improve consistency, and support faster collections follow-up.
