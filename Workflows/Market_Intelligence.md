# 📂 03 - Market Intelligence Workflow

- **Business function** : Finance / Account Planning / Market Intelligence
- **Trigger**: Annual client report and monthly external market reports are available for planning review
- **Prompts in this section**: P06, P07

---

## Section Purpose

This section supports client planning and budgeting by combining client-specific research with broader market intelligence. The process begins with analysing the client’s annual report to identify IT strategy signals, digital priorities, and possible indicators of IT spending direction. It then extends to synthesising multiple external market reports to understand the latest IT trends and likely areas of spending across the broader market. Together, these prompts help finance and planning teams prepare more informed budgeting assumptions, account plans, and internal planning discussions.
