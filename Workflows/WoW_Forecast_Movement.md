# 06 — Weekly Forecast Movement Review Workflow

- **Business function**: Finance / Forecasting / Performance Review
- **Trigger**: Current and prior week sales forecast files are available for comparison
- **Prompts in this section**: P09

# Section Purpose

This section supports the weekly forecast movement review workflow by helping finance teams compare current and prior week sales forecasts across all accounts and flag the accounts with material increases or decreases. In a recurring weekly review cycle, manually comparing forecast files can be time-consuming and may lead to inconsistent prioritisation of important movements. This prompt helps standardise week-over-week forecast comparison, improve visibility of significant changes, and support faster investigation of the accounts requiring attention.
